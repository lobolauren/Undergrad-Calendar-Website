usage: makegraph [-c <code> | -d <code> | -p <code>] [-o <filename>]

makes a graph showing the connections of different courses through their prerequisites.

allows the user to get a graph of an individual course and its prerequisites as well as 
a graph for an entire department as well as a degree program and its requirements

can only provide one of the following three options
[-c <code>]         automatically looks to graph specific course
[-d <code>]         automatically looks to graph specific department
[-p <code>]         automatically looks to graph specific program

[-o <filename>]     specifies the output filename
